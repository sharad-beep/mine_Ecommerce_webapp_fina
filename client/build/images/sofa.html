    AVALON GREY FABRIC RECLINER

    Duroflex Avalon Fabric Recliner is your go to place for the lazy days. Made from trusted Duroflex foam and premium upholstered fabric, it is designed for comfort. Sit, binge watch, or read! With 3 reclining settings, you could literally live in it!
    The back has nylon webbing and a zig-zag spring, it is filled non-siliconized virgin polyfill for a good back support. The armrest features additional polyfill cushion to make your long hours of sitting a breeze.
    The sturdy pine wood frame retains its shape for a long time. Our recliner is certified for European norms of durability.

    Ergonomic Design
